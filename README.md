## Bright Car Sales Analysis

## Project Description / Overview

The Bright Car Sales Analysis project focuses on analyzing vehicle sales data to uncover business insights related to sales performance, revenue generation, profitability, mileage trends, and regional performance.

The project involved cleaning and transforming raw vehicle sales data using SQL, followed by creating analytical visuals and dashboards using Excel and Google Looker Studio. The analysis supports business decision-making by identifying top-performing car brands, profitable regions, and pricing patterns.

The dataset contains over 558,000 vehicle sales records collected between April 2015 and May 2015.

## Aim of the Project

The aim of this project was to:

- Analyze vehicle sales performance
- Identify profitable vehicles and regions
- Understand pricing and mileage relationships
- Generate actionable business insights
- Build interactive dashboards for decision-making support

## Summary of Results

Key findings from the analysis include:

- Total vehicle sales recorded: 558,811
- Unique vehicles sold: 550,296
- Ford had the highest number of listings, followed by Chevrolet and Nissan
- Sales data ranged from 03 April 2015 to 27 May 2015
- Some records contained missing values in make, model, selling price, and sale date columns
- High-profit vehicles generated stronger profit margins above 10%
- Regional analysis revealed differences in sales performance across states
- Mileage had a noticeable impact on vehicle selling prices
- Revenue and profitability trends helped identify top-performing vehicle categories

## Conclusion

The Bright Car Sales Analysis project successfully transformed raw vehicle sales data into meaningful business insights through SQL analysis and dashboard visualization.

The project demonstrated the importance of data cleaning, transformation, and visualization in supporting business decisions. Insights generated from the analysis can help dealerships improve pricing strategies, identify profitable regions, optimize inventory decisions, and enhance overall sales performance.

The dashboards and analytical findings provide a strong foundation for future business intelligence and data-driven decision-making initiatives.
